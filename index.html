Health & Wellness Website

Overview / Context

This website is for exploring some cool technology and interesting tools used in healthcare. These can go from online platforms or websites to physical equipment.

By understanding some of these tools, you can get a better idea of how new or already existing ideas can save lives and improves health management overall.

Part A: Existing Technology

For example, an online platform like GoodRx is good for patients or people to find lower prices for prescriptions and is well known of quick navigation and people finding it easy to use.

Now, a physical idea, still hardware, like 3d printing is starting to get more popular within healthcare. This type of technology is currently being used to do things like making prosthetics, custom medications, and even helps by making treatment more effective.

Part B: Future Technology

Future things, like AI can or will likely more on focus on better accuracy like diagnosing or finding diseases and gene editing to fix genetic conditions. It could also possibly calculate complex or more confusing dosages quicker and speed up some things for drug discovery. This can help people or scientists more over to find cures for diseases than humans can alone.

Nanotechnology is when you use microscopic nanobots to deliver medicine right into the sick cells without affecting the rest of somebody's body. In the future these tiny machines could perform different repairs or kill cancer cells, making treatments more successful.

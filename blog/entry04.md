## Content 

For blog 4, I chose <b>Animate.css</b> as my tool. Animate.css is a website that lets you try different basic animations. These include:

* Different exit animations (Rotating exits, fading exits, bouncing exits, etc)
* Different entrance animations (Back entrances, sliding entrances, zooming entrances, etc).



 I tinkered with Animate.css by clicking, previewing and seeing what all the animations on the website did. I just played around and tested the animations on the website itself. Even without applying them to a the actual page, I enjoyed the effects and the changed feel of the animations. This helped me get an idea of the different options I could choose from and what I can use in the future for an intended website if I decide on it or if im assigned work for it.
